2. Introduction

2.1 Problem Statement

2.2 Overview

Breast cancer patients often face numerous challenges throughout their precaution, curing, and recovery journey, including medication management, maintaining healthy habits, managing emotional well-being, adhering to dietary recommendations, and seeking support from others.

Existing solutions lack a comprehensive approach to addressing these diverse needs, leading to fragmented care experiences and decreased quality of life for patients. Therefore, there is a critical need for an integrated mobile application that provides personalized support, reminders, educational resources, and a supportive community platform tailored specifically to the needs of breast cancer patients, empowering them to navigate their journey with confidence, resilience, and improved overall well-being.

2.3 ‚ÄúPeony‚Äù & Value Proposition

Peony, symbolizes resilience, beauty, and renewal in the face of adversity. Just as the peony's petals unfold to reveal layers of intricate elegance, the Peony app blossoms with knowledge, comfort, and support for breast cancer patients. It bridges the gap between fear and hope, offering a sanctuary where anxiety is soothed by the gentle touch of reliable information and compassionate community.

In addition, the peony flower looks like a breast tissue visually (in an abstract way).

Value Proposition:

Peony is for Breast Cancer Prognosis, Prevention, and Patient Care.

Pink stands for Power.

2.4 Key Features

  • Personalized Information Hub: Tailored content recommendations based on user preferences and stage of the breast cancer journey.
  • Treatment Tracker: Reminders for appointments, medication schedules, and symptom monitoring.
  • Support Network: Peer-to-peer support groups, expert-led forums, and one-on-one chat functionality.
  • Wellness Resources: Meditation guides, stress management techniques, and exercise routines designed for patients undergoing treatment.
  • Educational Modules: Interactive modules covering topics such as diagnosis, treatment options, side effect management, and survivorship.
  • Community Events: Virtual support sessions, educational webinars, and fundraising initiatives organized within the app.

2.5 Objectives

Information Accessibility: Ensure easy access to accurate and up-to-date information regarding breast cancer prevention, diagnosis, treatment options, and recovery strategies.

Emotional Support: Offer personalized support resources, including peer-to-peer connections, mental health guidance, and coping mechanisms, to reduce anxiety and promote emotional well-being.

Treatment Management: Facilitate efficient management of treatment schedules, medication reminders, and symptom tracking to empower patients in their healthcare journey.

Community Engagement: Foster a supportive community where users can share experiences, seek advice, and find solidarity with others facing similar challenges.

Empowerment and Advocacy: Empower users to become advocates for their own health by promoting self-advocacy, encouraging proactive communication with healthcare providers, and facilitating participation in research initiatives.

5.1 Product-wise

  • Bridge Information Gaps: Provide reliable and comprehensive information.
  • Holistic Approach: An integrated APP that address the physical and psychological aspects of the fight against cancer.
  • Empower Users: Provide strong support system for breast cancer patients.

5.2 Design-wise (Design Brief)

Peony is a an integrated and intuitive APP designed to bridge the information gap and alleviate anxiety among breast cancer patients and potential patients throughout their journey of precaution, treatment, and recovery. Recognizing the multifaceted challenges individuals face during this sensitive time, Peony aims to provide comprehensive support, reliable information, and a sense of empowerment through user-friendly features and accessible resources.
